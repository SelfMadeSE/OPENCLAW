# Round‑Table Lanes Preflight — Risk Memo (Fast‑Cash Outreach & Public Campaigns)
**Date:** 2026‑04‑22 (MDT)
**Context:** Current “hold‑state and approval boundaries” are unspecified in this subagent scope. This memo is conservative and flags items that require explicit approval, legal/compliance review, or verification of facts/permissions.

---
## Lane 1: Fast‑Cash Outreach (Sales/Revenue‑Generating Actions)

### ✅ GREEN (safe to greenlight now)
- **Internal planning & enablement**
  - Define target ICPs, outreach sequences, value props, and qualification criteria.
  - Draft messaging templates (email/DM/call scripts) **without** sending.
  - Build prospect lists from approved sources (opt‑in lists, existing CRM, public business contact data where allowed).
  - Internal training, role‑plays, and QA review of scripts.
- **Non‑public assets**
  - One‑pagers, pitch decks, pricing sheets **clearly labeled “internal draft.”**
  - Landing page wireframes or copy drafts not yet published.

### ⚠️ YELLOW (proceed only with explicit approval or verification)
- **Outbound outreach to new contacts**
  - Requires confirmation of **compliance** (CAN‑SPAM/anti‑spam, privacy laws, platform TOS) and list provenance.
  - Requires **brand approval** of claims, pricing, or guarantees.
- **Limited pilot outreach**
  - Small‑batch sending to validate conversion, **only** if approvals exist and compliance verified.
- **Affiliate/partner offers**
  - Any rev‑share or referral outreach should be reviewed for contract/terms alignment.

### ❌ RED (do not proceed without higher authorization)
- **Cold outreach at scale** without verified list sources and compliance sign‑off.
- **Claims of guaranteed results, ROI, or performance** without evidence/legal approval.
- **Using restricted data sources** (scraped personal emails, purchased lists without consent).
- **Price or contract commitments** on behalf of the org without approval.

## Lane 2: Memorable Public‑Facing Content/Campaign Work

### ✅ GREEN (safe to greenlight now)
- **Concepting & internal drafts**
  - Campaign themes, storyboards, scripts, visuals, or ad copy **kept internal.**
  - Mood boards and creative direction decks.
- **Evergreen brand‑safe content**
  - Company mission/values summaries, non‑specific thought leadership drafts.
  - Educational content without product performance claims.
- **Asset inventory & rights audit**
  - Verify existing images/video/music licenses and usage rights.

### ⚠️ YELLOW (needs explicit approval or verification)
- **Public posts, ads, or press‑facing copy**
  - Requires approval for tone, brand alignment, and legal compliance.
- **Case studies / testimonials**
  - Must verify consent, usage rights, and factual accuracy.
- **Competitive comparisons**
  - Must be factual, documented, and legally reviewed.

### ❌ RED (do not proceed without higher authorization)
- **Publishing public campaigns** involving:
  - Unverified claims, outcomes, or customer endorsements.
  - Use of third‑party IP (music, images, logos) without licenses.
  - Regulatory‑sensitive claims (health, finance, legal outcomes).
  - Sensitive or controversial messaging without executive approval.
