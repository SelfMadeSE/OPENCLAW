# 2026-04-19 Memory Note

Black Box Global roundtable decision: favor a real, narrow, monetizable business over fantasy. Vote was for a productized AI + automation studio centered on a 7-day workflow sprint for small businesses.

What to build in 7 days: one-page site, one offer, simple intake/delivery system, CRM pipeline, outbound list, and 2 internal demo case studies.

Brand/site should feel like a calm expensive systems company, with charcoal/off-white/electric blue, sharp grid, real screenshots, and a direct no-hype tone.

Realistic 8-month numbers: start near $0-$3k, then progress to $4k-$8k/month, $8k-$15k/month, and $12k-$25k/month by months 7-8, with a base case around $15k/month if outreach and delivery stay focused.

Build-in-public should feel real by showing actual work, before/after screenshots, lessons, mistakes, and specific client outcomes, not inflated founder mythology.

- Media (optional): 1–2 episodes documenting real pilot outcomes

**Realistic 8-month numbers (base case):** 60 clients at $299/mo by month 8 → **~$18k MRR** (upside 80–100 clients → $24k–$30k MRR; downside 15–25 clients → $4.5k–$7.5k MRR).

## Media vote (SIGNAL)

**Vote:** YouTube helps, but only as a sales asset. It should support the core business, not become the business.

**7-day media plan:**
- Day 1, lock offer + CTA
- Day 2, film the core demo
- Day 3, publish 1 YouTube case-study video
- Day 4, cut 5 shorts + 2 social posts
- Day 5, publish 2 proof posts with screenshots
- Day 6, send clips to outbound leads
- Day 7, review replies/calls and double down on the best hook

**Creative direction:**
- Titles should be specific, practical, and outcome-led
- Thumbnails should be simple, high-contrast, and proof-first
- Clips should be short, reusable, and tied to sales objections

**Revenue role:** media lowers trust friction, improves outbound conversion, and gives the offer receipts. It is a distribution layer, not the core business.

**8-month view:** realistic path is $0-$3k early, then $4k-$8k/mo, then $8k-$15k/mo, with $12k-$25k/mo possible by months 7-8 if the business stays focused and the content is built from real client work.
