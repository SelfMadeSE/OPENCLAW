## KPI Formulas

- **Outreach → Reply Rate**: replies / outreach_sent × 100 (target: 10%+)
- **Reply → Call Rate**: calls / replies × 100 (target: 40%+)
- **Call → Close Rate**: deals / calls × 100 (target: 30%+)
- **Average Deal Value**: total_revenue / deals_closed
- **Revenue Per Outreach**: total_revenue / outreach_sent (target: $5+)
- **Time to First Dollar**: days from first outreach to first payment
