## SEQUENCE 1: "Free Website Audit" (3 Emails)

### Email 1: The Hook
**Subject Line Options** (A/B test):
- A: "Quick question about [BUSINESS NAME]'s website"
- B: "I noticed something about [BUSINESS NAME].com"
- C: "[FIRST NAME], your website might be costing you customers"

**Body**:
```
Hi [FIRST NAME],

I was looking at [BUSINESS NAME]'s website and noticed a few things
that might be costing you customers — specifically on mobile.

I put together a quick 2-minute audit for you (free, no strings).
Here's what I found:

[INSERT 2-3 SPECIFIC OBSERVATIONS]
- Example: "Your homepage takes 6.2 seconds to load on mobile —
  53% of visitors leave after 3 seconds"
- Example: "Your contact form is below the fold on phones —
  most visitors never scroll that far"
- Example: "No Google Business Profile link, which hurts local SEO"

I help small businesses like yours turn their website into a
customer-generating machine. If you're interested, I'd love to
show you what a refreshed site could look like — I can mock up
a homepage concept in 48 hours, no charge.

Either way, hope the audit helps.

Best,
Rylee Benson
Web Design & Brand Strategy
[PORTFOLIO LINK]
```

**Send timing**: Tuesday or Wednesday, 9-11 AM recipient's local time
**Wait**: 3 business days before Email 2

**Subject**: Re: Quick question about [BUSINESS NAME]'s website

**Body**:
```

in weeks, not months. My clients typically see 30-50% more inquiries
within the first month of launching a redesigned site.

I have one opening this month. If you'd like a free mockup of what
your new site could look like, just reply "interested" and I'll
put one together.

No pressure either way.

Rylee
```

## SEQUENCE 2: "Reddit/Forum Response" (For inbound leads)

### Template: Someone Posts "I Need a Website"

**Body**:
```
Hey! I actually build websites for small businesses — happy to help.

A few quick questions so I can give you a real estimate:

1. What does your business do? (industry/niche)
2. Do you have a current site, or starting from scratch?
3. What's the main goal? (get more calls, sell products, look professional)
4. Rough timeline?

For context, most of my projects land between $500-$2,000 depending
on complexity. That gets you a custom responsive site with SEO setup,
contact forms, and analytics — not a template with your logo slapped on.

Happy to show you some examples if you're interested. No pressure.
```
