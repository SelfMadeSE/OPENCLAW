# Article 13 Soft 404 Fix — 2026-04-29

## Root Cause

**Slug mismatch between data layer and internal link references.**

The blog post "From Audit to Booking: The Full Service Business Workflow" was registered with slug `"audit-to-booking-workflow"` in both:
- `lib/blog-posts.ts` (data layer)
- `app/blog/[slug]/page.tsx` (component map key)

However, the post itself (in `AuditToBookingPost.tsx`) internally links to `/blog/from-audit-to-booking` and the series list refers to "Article 13" at that same URL path. The SEO-expected URL was `/blog/from-audit-to-booking`, not `/blog/audit-to-booking-workflow`.

At runtime, navigating to `/blog/from-audit-to-booking`:
1. Next.js `generateStaticParams` did NOT include `from-audit-to-booking` (it generated `audit-to-booking-workflow`)
2. `generateMetadata` received `params.slug = "from-audit-to-booking"` but `getBlogPost("from-audit-to-booking")` returned `undefined`
3. This fell through to the soft 404 rendering

The route was partially recognized because Next.js routed to `[slug]/page.tsx`, but the metadata lookup failed, triggering the `notFound()` path.
