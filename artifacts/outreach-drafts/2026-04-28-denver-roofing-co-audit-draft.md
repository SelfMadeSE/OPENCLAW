## Specific Site Findings Used in Outreach

1. **No online inspection booking** — For a roofing company, the single highest-intent action a visitor can take is booking a free inspection or estimate. Denver Roofing Co. has no online scheduling tool whatsoever — all bookings must happen via phone during business hours.

2. **No live chat or instant quote tool** — Competitor roofing sites offer instant quote calculators (roof size, material type → rough estimate) and live chat. This site has neither, meaning visitors can't get quick answers or rough pricing without calling.

3. **No city-specific landing pages** — The site has well-written general content but no individual pages for the specific Denver suburbs it serves. Competitors who rank for "roofing in [suburb]" are capturing those search terms.

4. **Great foundation, missing conversion layer** — The WordPress design is clean, content is thorough, and service pages exist (asphalt, metal, tile). But the site functions as a brochure rather than a lead generation machine.

5. **No Google Reviews or testimonials prominently displayed** — Despite having satisfied customers, no review widget or testimonial section is visible on the homepage or service pages.
