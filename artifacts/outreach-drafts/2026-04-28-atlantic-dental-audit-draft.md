## Specific Site Findings Used in Outreach

1. **No online appointment booking** — The most fundamental conversion tool for a dental practice. Every competitor in Denver offers it. Without it, the practice is losing after-hours bookings entirely.

2. **No contact form, no phone visible on homepage** — The website has no way for a new patient to actually reach the practice. The content is wall-of-text about general dentistry with zero conversion mechanisms.

3. **No new patient offers, no insurance info** — No new patient special, no accepted insurance list, no financing information. Dental patients typically search for these before calling.

4. **No dentist bios, no practice credentials** — No "Meet the Doctor" page. For a healthcare provider, this is a major trust gap. Patients want to know who's treating them.

5. **Wall-of-text content with no visual structure** — The homepage is a single paragraph block of generic dentistry copy. No images, no video, no visual hierarchy.
